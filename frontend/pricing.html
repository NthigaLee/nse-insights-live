Frequently asked

Can I switch between card and M-Pesa later?

Yes. Change payment method any time in your account settings. Your subscription stays active during the switch.

How does M-Pesa billing work?

The first charge is an STK push you approve on your handset. For monthly renewals we send a payment request 2 days before expiry via the Safaricom paybill. You can set up standing authorisation for fully automatic renewals.

Is there a student or analyst discount?

Yes — 50% off Trader and Professional with a valid student or CFA candidate ID. Email students@nseinsights.co.ke to apply.

Do you offer a refund?

If you cancel within 7 days of your first payment and haven't exported data, we'll refund in full. After that, cancellations stop future charges but don't refund the current period.

Where does the data come from?

Prices come from the Nairobi Securities Exchange. Financials are sourced from company-filed annual and half-year reports and reconciled against CMA filings. We update within 24 hours of publication.

Can I get an invoice for my company?

Absolutely. Every payment generates a VAT invoice with your company name and KRA PIN. Access them from Account → Billing history.
